Background

The Haitian Global Health Alliance is a nonprofit delivering critical healthcare services and social support to low-income Haitians. To combat violence against children, HGHA developed an educational program for Haitian men and women across various walks of life — including teachers, parents of first- and second-graders, and community leaders.

Objective

Given survey data on attitudes toward violence against children before and after the educational program in a spreadsheet, visualize trends in the percentage of correct responses. Group by respondents' gender, profession, and violence type. Create interactive dashboards for each violence type, plus three additional charts with cross-filter capability.

Outcome

Delivered a fully interactive Tableau Public dashboard suite covering multiple violence categories (emotional, physical, gang, sexual). Enabled HGHA staff and stakeholders to quickly identify which groups showed the most improvement and where additional outreach was most needed.
